The Eiffel Tower, Paris

The Eiffel Tower, a global cultural icon of France, stands at 300 meters tall. It offers stunning views of the Paris skyline and is a must-visit for every traveler.

Vieux-Port, Marseille

The Vieux-Port (Old Port) is the historical heart of Marseille. This vibrant area is lined with cafés and restaurants, offering a picturesque view of the harbor.

Louvre Museum, Paris

The Louvre is the world’s largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris. It houses thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

Calanques National Park, Marseille

Calanques National Park features stunning cliffs, crystal-clear waters, and breathtaking views. It’s perfect for hiking, swimming, and enjoying the natural beauty of the Mediterranean.
